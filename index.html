Elizabeth MacInnis is a Canadian born singer-songwriter and performer who lends her 30+ years of experience and talent to vocal coaching.

Her rich and varied musical upbringing began in musical theatre at a young age. She was bitten by the music bug, therefore, realizing her passion.

Discovering early, she had a 5-octave range that led to a long resume of performing and recording in a variety of musical genres. From Choir–light Opera to Jazz-Dixieland, Country, Rock, Pop, and Soul, she blends these different styles to express her unique sound.

You will find many studio recordings of her work as an original solo artist and as a main or backing vocalist for other artists. You may also recognize her stunning voice in commercials and television.

Session Structure

    Vocal Coaching for Beginners (Ages: 10 - adult)

  • Where does your voice sit?
  • Ear training (learn to really hear yourself)
  • Correct breathing and vocal use
  • Cultivate your most sincere and finest performance
  • Discover your uniqueness

    Vocal Coaching for Advanced Singers (Ages: 10 - Adult)

  • Brush up on technique
  • Fine tune your style
  • Identify & cultivate your stage ‘persona’
  • Identify & correct any bad habits
  • Stretch your range
